AI Sales Intelligence Engine Architecture
Layer 1 — Internet Discovery Engine
This layer discovers companies automatically.
Sources crawled:
company websites
public directories
press releases
job boards
government business registries
conference speaker lists
startup databases
Crawler tools typically use:
Playwright
Scrapy
Headless Chromium
Crawler tasks:
discover domains
extract company name
detect company type
capture contact pages
Output:
company name
website
location
industry
employee estimate
Layer 2 — AI Entity Extraction
Once pages are crawled, AI extracts structured information.
AI reads:
team pages
blog posts
press releases
PDFs
conference bios
Example extraction:
Name: Sarah Kim
Role: VP of Operations
Company: NorthBridge Logistics
Location: Dallas
Tools used:
LLM extraction models
NER (Named Entity Recognition)
semantic parsing
Database objects created:
Company
Contact
Role
Department
Layer 3 — Organization Graph
Instead of storing flat lists, modern systems build a relationship graph.
Example structure:
Company
  ↓
Department
  ↓
Employee
  ↓
Role
This allows queries like:
Find CTOs at SaaS companies with 50-200 employees
Graph databases often used:
Neo4j
TypeDB
ArangoDB
Layer 4 — Email Pattern Intelligence
AI learns email structures by domain.
Example domain:
northbridge.com
Possible patterns:
first@domain
first.last@domain
first_initial_last@domain
Verification methods:
SMTP handshake
domain MX validation
deliverability prediction
This produces verified business emails.
Layer 5 — Signal Detection Engine
Sales teams care about buying signals.
Signals detected by monitoring:
hiring pages
funding announcements
leadership changes
new product launches
tech stack changes
Example signal:
Company hiring 5 DevOps engineers
→ high probability infrastructure spending
Signal database:
Hiring signal
Funding signal
Leadership signal
Technology signal
Layer 6 — Technographic Scanner
Your crawler can detect software used by companies.
By scanning websites for:
JavaScript libraries
tracking pixels
API calls
cdn references
Example detection:
HubSpot
Shopify
Stripe
Salesforce
AWS
This is called technographic data.
Layer 7 — Lead Scoring AI
AI ranks leads automatically.
Example scoring model:
industry fit
company size
growth signals
job seniority
technology stack
engagement
Example output:
Score: 91/100
Reason:
• hiring IT team
• uses AWS
• new CTO
Layer 8 — Outreach Automation
Once leads are identified, outreach begins.
Automated sequences:
email
LinkedIn connection
follow-ups
meeting booking
AI personalization uses:
company news
recent announcements
industry context
Layer 9 — CRM Sync
Your engine pushes leads to a CRM like:
Salesforce
HubSpot
Example flow:
Lead discovered
↓
Email verified
↓
Score assigned
↓
Lead pushed to CRM
↓
Sales pipeline created
