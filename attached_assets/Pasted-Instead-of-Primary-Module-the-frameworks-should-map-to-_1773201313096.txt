HOW THIS CONNECTS TO YOUR APP
Resource Hub
Stores the frameworks.
Diagnostics
Runs the frameworks.
Systems
Bundles frameworks into automated operating systems.
Advisory
Explains why something matters and what to do next.
Dashboard
Shows the results of frameworks and diagnostics.
Initiatives
Where recommended actions appear.
Action Items
Where execution tasks are generated.
Reports
Exports analysis results.
